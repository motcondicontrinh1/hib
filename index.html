Platform Features

Everything Your
70.3 Training Needs

Six core capabilities — each one dialed for the exact demands of half-iron distance racing.

Tri-Sport Analytics
Unified dashboard tracking swim pace, bike power, and run form with discipline-specific metrics and transition analysis.
Power Zone Training
FTP-based zones 1–7 with automatic zone detection, normalized power calculations, and intensity factor tracking per ride.
Brick Intelligence
Dedicated brick workout builder that models the bike-to-run physiological shift and tracks your off-bike running efficiency over time.
Race Day Planner
Hour-by-hour race strategy with pace targets, nutrition timing, heart-rate ceilings, and contingency plans for heat or wind.
Course Intelligence
Elevation-adjusted power targets, course-specific interval sets, and real race route uploads so you simulate race day in training.
Recovery Tracking
HRV trends, sleep quality scores, and daily readiness ratings integrated into your training load to prevent overreach injuries.
